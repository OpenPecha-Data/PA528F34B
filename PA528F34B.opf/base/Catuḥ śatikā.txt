21. śatruvat yānti te kālā niyamena kṣaṇādayaḥ|sarvvathā tena te rāgaḥ śatrubhūteṣu teṣu mā||21||

22. viprayogabhayādgehānna nirgacchami [durmmate]|[vivicya] nāma kartavyaṃ kuryyāddaṇḍena ko
budhaḥ||22||

7. śarīraṃ sucireṇāpi sukhasya svaṃ na jāyate|pareṇābhibhavo nāma svabhāvasya na yujyate||32||

8. agrayāṇāṃ mānasaṃ duḥkhamitareṣāṃ śarīrajam|duḥkhadvayena lokoyamahanyahani hanyate||33||

9. kalpanāyāḥ sukhaṃ vaśyaṃ vaśyādduḥkhasya kalpanā|atosti kiñcit sarvvatra na duḥkhādvalamantaram||34||

10. kālo yathā yathā yāti duḥkhavṛddhistatha tathā|tasmāt kaḍevarasyāsya paravadṛśyate sukham||35||

11. vyādhayo'nye ca dṛśyante yāvanto duḥkhahetavaḥ|tāvanto na tu dṛśyante narāṇāṃ sukhahetavaḥ||36||

12. sukhasya varddhamānasya yathā dṛṣṭo viparyayaḥ|duḥkhasya varddhamānasya tathā nāsti viparyayaḥ||37||

13. pratināsikayā tuṣṭiḥ syāddhīnāṅgasya kasyacit|rāgo'śucipratīkāre puṣpādāviṣyate tathā||73||

24. śuci nāma ca tadyuktaṃ vairāgyaṃ yatra jāyate|na ca so'sti kvacidbhāvo niyayād rāgakāraṇam||74||

25. anityamaśubhaṃ duḥkhamanātmeti catuṣṭayam|ekasminneva sarvāṇi sambharvānta samāsataḥ||75||

1. ahaṃ mameti vā darpaḥ sataḥ kasya bhaved bhave|yasmāt sarvve'pi sāmānyā viṣayāḥ sarvva dehinām||76||

2. gaṇadāsasya te darpaḥ ṣaḍbhāgena bhṛtasya kaḥ|jāyate'dhikṛte kāryyamāyattaṃ yatra tatra vā||77||

14. ṛṣīṇāṃ ceṣṭitaṃ sarvva kurvīta na vicakṣaṇaḥ|hīnamadhyaviśiṣṭatvaṃ yasmātteṣvapi vidyate||89||

15. putravat pālito lokaḥ purataḥ pārthivaiḥ śubhaiḥ|mṛgāraṇyīkṛtaḥ so'dya kalidharmasamāśritaiḥ||90||

16. chidraprahāriṇaḥ pāpaṃ yadi rājño na vidyate|anyeṣāmapi caurāṇāṃ tat prāgeva na vidyate||91||

17. sarvvasvasya parityāgo madyādiṣu na pūjitaḥ|ātmano'pi parityāgaḥ kiṃ manye pūjito raṇe||92||

23. vipro'pi karmmanā śūdraḥ kena manye na jāyate||98||

24. pāpasyaiśvaryyavadrājan saṃvibhāgo na vidyate|vidvānnāma parasyārthe kaḥ kuryyādāyatovadhaṃ||99||

25. dṛṣṭvā samān viśiṣṭāṃśca parāṃśchaktisamanvitān|aiśvāryajanito mānaḥ satāṃ hṛdi na tiṣṭhati||100||

1. na ceṣṭā kila buddhānāmasti kācidakāraṇā|niḥśvāso'pi hitāyaiva prāṇināṃ saṃpravarttatte||101||
